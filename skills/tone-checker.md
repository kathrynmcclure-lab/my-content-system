# Skill: Tone Checker

Review content for tone, clarity, and consistency.

## Usage

Invoke this skill when you need to evaluate the tone of written content.

## Instructions

1. Read the content provided
2. Evaluate for:
   - Tone (formal, informal, neutral, etc.)
   - Clarity and readability
   - Consistency with project voice/style
   - Potential issues (too harsh, too passive, ambiguous, etc.)
3. Provide specific, actionable feedback
4. Suggest rewrites where needed
